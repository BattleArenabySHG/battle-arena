Game Guidelines

  • This is a turn-based game.
  • Only one player plays at a time.
  • Wait for your turn before playing.
  • The player with the highest score wins.
  • Do not refresh the page during the game.
  • Initial Health Points (HP) of each player is 100
  • Attack will reduces 10HP for other players if they didn't select defend
  • Heavy Attack reduces 20HP for other players if they didn't select defend
  • Defend will not reduce any Health Points
  • There will be only 2 Heavy attacks,3 Defends and unlimited Attacks
